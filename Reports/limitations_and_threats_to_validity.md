# Limitations and Threats to Validity

1. The executable experiment uses the available matched yearly/suffix data in the current project state.
2. The selected-line protocol is used for computational feasibility and should not be presented as a full-grid deployment benchmark.
3. Raw longitude and latitude are excluded from training features to avoid geographic identifier effects; they are used only for metadata-based visualization.
4. B-TGPRF may not dominate every individual metric; claims must be evidence-based and metric-specific.
5. Calibration, thresholds, and component choices are validation-derived and must not be retuned on test or external-test partitions.
